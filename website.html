About Us

Qhanna Gooden

My name is Qhanna Gooden and I am 17 years old. I am a senior at John Bowne High School. The summer before senior year i attended Girls Who Code at Weight Watchers. There I learned scratch,python,json and arduino. As our final project Anneke, Smruthi and I decided to make a website that would show what's happening in the world today. All over the world and not just in our backyard. We believe that this website will give people a better chance to access information about what's going on in the world. Without bias os lies. We hope that you all enjoy our little project and hopefully the more to come.

Smruthi Srinivasan

My name is Smruthi Srinivasan and I live in Edison, New Jersey. I am a rising senior who attends John P. Stevens High School. I am part of Girls Who Code and enjoy computer science and coding. Some of my hobbies include dance and playing the electronic keyboard. I enjoyed making this website with my team and we hope that it raises more awareness about local and global issues as well as makes it easier to access news from different sources!
